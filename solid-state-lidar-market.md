<p><strong>The Solid-State LiDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS Based Scanning</li><li>Phase Array</li><li>Non-Scanning Flash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solid-State LiDAR market includes three main types: MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) based scanning, which utilizes small mirrors to direct laser beams for 3D mapping; phased array, employing multiple fixed laser sources that can electronically steer beams for rapid data capture; and non-scanning flash LiDAR, which emits a broad pulse of light to capture entire scenes at once. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of resolution, speed, and application suitability, serving industries such as automotive, robotics, and surveying.</p></p>
