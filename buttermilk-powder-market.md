<p><strong>The Buttermilk Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Dried Buttermilk Powder</li><li>Atmospheric Roller Dried Buttermilk Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The buttermilk powder market is segmented into two primary types: spray dried buttermilk powder and atmospheric roller dried buttermilk powder. Spray dried buttermilk powder is produced through a process that rapidly dries liquid buttermilk using hot air, resulting in fine particles and a longer shelf life. In contrast, atmospheric roller dried buttermilk powder is made by spreading liquid buttermilk onto heated rollers, which produces thicker flakes. Both types offer versatility in food products, enhancing flavor and nutritional content in various applications.</p></p>
