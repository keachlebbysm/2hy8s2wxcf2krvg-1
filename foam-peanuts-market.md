<p><p>The foam peanuts market is divided into two main types: biodegradable foam peanuts and regular foam peanuts. Biodegradable foam peanuts are made from natural materials like cornstarch, allowing them to decompose more easily in the environment, making them a sustainable packaging option. In contrast, regular foam peanuts are typically made from expanded polystyrene (EPS), which offers excellent cushioning and lightweight protection but poses environmental challenges due to its non-biodegradable nature. These distinctions cater to varying consumer preferences for sustainability and performance in packaging.</p></p>
