<p><p>The Medium Pet Bird Feeds market is categorized into two main types: staple and supplement feeds. Staple feeds serve as the primary diet, providing essential nutrients and energy needed for a bird's daily health and well-being. These often include seeds, pellets, and formulated diets tailored to specific bird species. Supplement feeds, on the other hand, enhance the staple diet by adding vitamins, minerals, or treats like fresh fruits and vegetables, boosting overall nutrition and encouraging natural foraging behaviors.</p></p>
