<p><p>The Sugar Mixes Market is divided into two main types: Natural Sugar Mixes and Sugar Substitute Mixes. </p><p>Natural Sugar Mixes primarily consist of blends derived from unrefined or minimally processed sugars, such as cane or coconut sugar, offering a more organic option. </p><p>In contrast, Sugar Substitute Mixes are formulated with low-calorie or non-caloric sweeteners, such as stevia or aspartame, aimed at reducing sugar intake while still providing sweetness. These options cater to health-conscious consumers seeking alternatives to traditional sugars.</p></p>
