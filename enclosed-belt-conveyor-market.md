<p><strong>The Enclosed Belt Conveyor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller</li><li>Spool</li><li>Air Cushion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The enclosed belt conveyor market comprises three main types: roller, spool, and air cushion conveyors. Roller conveyors utilize rollers to support the belt, enabling smooth material movement while minimizing friction. Spool conveyors involve a belt wound around spools, facilitating compact design and easy material handling. Air cushion conveyors use a thin layer of air between the belt and the conveying surface, reducing friction and allowing for high-speed transport of materials. Each type offers unique advantages for various industrial applications.</p></p>
