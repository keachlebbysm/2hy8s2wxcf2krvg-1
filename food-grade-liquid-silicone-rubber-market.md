<p><p>Food Grade Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) is categorized based on viscosity levels, which influence its applications and processing. Low viscosity LSR is easy to pour and mold, ideal for intricate designs and high-speed production. Medium viscosity offers a balance between flow and elasticity, suitable for a range of products like kitchenware and seals. High viscosity LSR, being thicker, is used in applications requiring durability and strength, such as bakeware and industrial components, ensuring safety and performance in food-related environments.</p></p>
