<p><p>The Flaxseed Oil nutrition market comprises various extraction methods, each impacting oil quality and demand. The Squeezing Method involves mechanically pressing flaxseeds to obtain oil, preserving nutrients. The Hot Pressing Method uses heat, enhancing yield but potentially degrading some nutrients. The Leaching Method employs solvents to extract oil, maximizing yield but raising concerns about residual chemicals. Each method caters to different consumer preferences, influencing market dynamics and positioning flaxseed oil as a versatile ingredient in health products and dietary supplements.</p></p>
